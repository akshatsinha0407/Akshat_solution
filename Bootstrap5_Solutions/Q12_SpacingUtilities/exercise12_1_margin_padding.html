Exercise 12.1 – Margin & Padding Utilities

Margin Examples
m-3 (margin all sides)
mt-4 (margin top)
mb-2 (margin bottom)
ms-5 (margin start/left)
mx-auto (centered)
Padding Examples
p-2 (padding all sides)
py-5 (padding top+bottom)
px-4 (padding left+right)
pt-3 ps-5 (padding top + start)
Spacing Scale: 0=0rem, 1=0.25rem, 2=0.5rem, 3=1rem, 4=1.5rem, 5=3rem
Format: {property}{sides}-{size} — e.g. mt-3, px-4, py-2
